Adsterra Ad Placement Guide

  • Header Banner: 728x90 leaderboard at top of every page
  • Below Title: 336x280 rectangle below page title
  • In Content 1 & 2: Native/Display ads within content area
  • After Results Table: Ad shown after the main results table
  • After Chart: Ad shown after the weekly chart section
  • Before Footer: 728x90 leaderboard above footer
  • Post Top/Middle/Bottom: Ads on daily post and article pages
  • Post Sidebar: 300x250 sidebar ad on post pages
  • Chart Top/Bottom: Ads on chart pages
  • News Top / Between Posts: Ads on news listing page
  • Popunder: Opens ad in background tab on click (high CPM)
  • Social Bar: Floating notification-style ad (Adsterra exclusive)
  • Native Banner: Blends with content, recommended widget style
  • In-Page Push: Push notification style within page (no subscription needed)
  • Interstitial: Full-screen overlay ad (use sparingly)
  • Mobile Sticky Banner: Fixed banner at bottom on mobile devices
